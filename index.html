V E R I F I C A T I O N

Links for Verification of Implemantation and Effectiveness. This action is to be taken by Auditors.

February 24, 2024 at 12:00AM This form has been closed

FIRST VERIFICATION

The RFA No. and Auditor ID required will be given in the email initiating this process. The system will email you regarding the status of your RFA. Once resolved, no further action is needed for the RFA. Otherwise, if RFA is not resolved, RFA shall be subjected to the second verification.

This form is scheduled to open on March 01, 2024.

SECOND VERIFICATION

The RFA No. and Auditor ID required will be given in the email initiating this process. The system will email you regarding the status of your RFA. Once resolved, no further action is needed for the RFA. Otherwise, if RFA is not resolved, RFA shall be subjected to the third and final verification.

This form is scheduled to open on March 12, 2024.

FINAL VERIFICATION

The RFA No. and Auditor ID required will be given in the email initiating this process. The system will email you regarding the status of your RFA. Once resolved, no further action is needed for the RFA. Otherwise, if RFA is still not resolved, RFA shall be reported to the top management.
